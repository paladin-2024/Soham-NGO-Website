We are Smile of Hope African Ministries

God is transforming the world through his primary agency which is the church therefore as a ministry we work with different local churches focusing on planting and revitalized churches, equipping church leaders, having a community bible study groups, building Christian schools, supporting orphans and widows, differently abled people and displaced people among others.

Our Mission

As SOHAM our mission is sharing the gospel in our communities in words and actions through equipping disciples, empowering individuals with skill development, entrepreneurship, health promotion, farming, strengthen the communities and other community based programs for the praise of His glorious name.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see Christ exalted, a world of hope and smile and overcoming material and spiritual poverty and living transformed lives.
